Conditional dispatch table tracks all the batches that were not passed by the normal food safety standard. They go through quality controlled measures, get concessioned and their dispatch process is tightly controlled. It houses the dispatch ID, batch ID, quantity dispatched, who concessioned and reasons for concessioning the batch. Handling instructions are noted down and where they are going is tracked.
